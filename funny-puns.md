# Funny Puns - Star Wars Edition

1. I find your lack of puns disturbing.
2. What is the internal temperature of a Tauntaun? Lukewarm.
3. Why did the Stormtrooper buy an iPhone? Because he couldn't find the Droid he was looking for.
4. What do you call a Jedi who loves Italian food? Obi-Wan Cannoli.
5. How does Darth Vader like his toast? On the Dark Side.
6. What do you call a potato that turned to the Dark Side? Vader Tots.
7. Why does Princess Leia keep her hair in buns? So it doesn't hang Solo.
8. What's the internal monologue of every Stormtrooper? "I have a bad feeling about this shot."
9. How do Ewoks communicate over long distances? Ewookie talkie.
10. What do you call Chewbacca when he has chocolate in his fur? A chocolate Wookiee.
11. Why did Anakin Skywalker cross the road? To get to the Dark Side.
12. What's a Jedi's favorite toy? A Yo-Yoda.
13. What does Kylo Ren serve at dinner? First Order cuisine.
14. Why did Han Solo marry Leia? Because she was his only hope.
15. What do you call a nervous Jedi? Qui-Gon Jittery.
16. What do you call it when only one Star Wars character laughs at your joke? A Solo chuckle.
17. Why couldn't the Jedi master use the internet? Too many pop-up Siths.
18. What did the Rancor say after eating a Jedi? "That hit different — with the Force."
19. Why is BB-8 always rolling around? Because he can't find his footing after going to the Dark Side.
20. What do you call stormtroopers playing Monopoly? The Game of Clones — everyone loses eventually.
